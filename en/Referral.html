Seize this lucrative opportunity
Given the high demand for short-stay accommodation in the UK, it’s clear that no single entity can meet this need. That’s why we’re extending an invitation to landlords to partner with us and seize this lucrative opportunity.

We rent most properties
We rent most properties After extended rental periods or unexpected circumstances, properties may require substantial reinvestment before being relisted. Leaving them vacant is not ideal. Often, we don’t mind investing on the renovation of our partner landlords’ properties. Feel free to reach out to us and find mutually beneficial solutions.

Legally compliant
You may observe that subletting is prohibited under the Assured Shorthold Tenancy Agreement. However, the agreement we enter into with our landlord partners is a Leasing Agreement, enabling us to work together within legal boundaries.

Guaranteed Rent
Before things take a turn for the worse, all tenants make the same promises. Our word alone isn't our only assurance. Legally, tenant rights do not safeguard us because we enter the contract as a company. This implies that our landlords have an easier path to evict us if we violate the contract. However, this situation has never occurred with us.

£0 Management fee, Wear & tear Covered
We offer our partner landlords a unique approach by eliminating management fees. Instead, we collaborate to determine a fair rent that benefits both parties. Our agreement includes covering all wear and tear to ensure hassle-free renting for our partner landlords, while providing guests with exceptional short stays. In the event of significant repairs, such as a malfunctioning boiler, we work closely with our landlord partners to resolve the issue smoothly. Mostly importantly, we return properties in rent-ready condition to our landlord partners.
