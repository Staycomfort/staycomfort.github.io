Staycomfort

Together, we create a warm and wonderful journey for travellers.

We are a serviced accommodation provider.

Staycomfort aspires to offer an exquisite haven of comfort and warmth, enveloping our esteemed guests in elegantly furnished accommodations.

We acknowledge the significant demand from travellers coming to and moving around the UK. We are pleased to collaborate with buy-to-let property owners to enjoy the benefits of this demand.

Given the high demand for short-stay accommodation in the UK, it’s clear that no single entity can meet this need. That’s why we’re extending an invitation to landlords to partner with us and seize this lucrative opportunity.

Want to know more about serviced accomdation in the UK? You might be interested in our blog posts.

We partner with Landlords.

Compared to traditional landlords who rent their properties to individual tenants, our partner landlords enjoy several significant benefits.

Guaranteed Rent
All tenants make promises, but we offer more than just words. We enter contracts as a company, not an individual, which means we aren’t protected by tenant rights. This gives our landlords an easier path to eviction should we breach the contract. However, we’re proud to say that this has never happened.

No Management Fee & Wear and Tear Covered
We provide a unique proposition to our partner landlords by eliminating management fees. Instead, we work together to agree on a fair rent that benefits both parties. Our agreement also covers all wear and tear, ensuring a hassle-free renting experience for our landlords and exceptional short stays for our guests. In the event of significant repairs, such as a malfunctioning boiler, we collaborate closely with our landlord partners to resolve the issue smoothly. Most importantly, we return properties in rent-ready condition to our landlords.
